Coffee

Freshly roasted coffee

A local cafe with branches in Kamifukuoka and Tsuruse, we specialise in a variety of high quality coffee beans freshly roasted in our cafes. ​Besides coffee, we have food menu with snacks, desserts and full meals for lunch and dinner. We also serve various teas, alcoholic and non-alcoholic beverages.

​Our shops have friendly and relaxed atmospheres.
